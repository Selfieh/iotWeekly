Ingar’s Osu Tournament Rulebook
(which is really just a page of how things work)

The Basics

This is a weekly tournament in which up to 15 players will face off to place first across 8 unique beatmaps.
These maps will be preselected by the host, and will range between 4*-7*.
Nomod is standard UNLESS preselected by the host (ex. HRHD on a 4* map)

Date: Friday & Saturday
Time: 9:30PM CST
Length: 30 min - 1 hour (depending on maps)

How Are Winners Selected?

Winners are determined by placement, not score or FC.
At the end of the tournament, each player’s placement across the 8 maps played will be tallied and the players with the highest overall placement will be crowned the winners of that tournament.
1st and 2nd Place - 1 month of osu supporter
This is the same for both Friday and Saturday, meaning that a total of 4 players can win each weekend.
Players that have won are excluded from winning again until 1 month has passed since they received their prize.

Plans For The Future

• Larger prize pool (Top 3 winners vs. Top 2)
